Flagship SOC Investigation

Tier-1 SOC Alert · Identity & Access Monitoring

Alert Scenario

Multiple failed authentication attempts followed by a successful login from a new geographic location on a privileged user account.

Initial Triage

Reviewed authentication logs, timestamps, source IPs, and MFA status. Assessed account privilege level and flagged the alert due to abnormal login behavior and identity misuse risk.

Investigation & Analysis

Correlated historical login activity, analyzed geolocation variance, validated access patterns against baseline behavior, and ruled out false positives.

Response & Escalation

Enforced password reset and MFA, reviewed permissions, monitored follow-up activity, and documented findings for Tier-2 / IAM escalation.

SOC Analyst Takeaway: Demonstrates Tier-1 alert triage, identity misuse detection, event correlation, and escalation-ready documentation aligned with real SOC workflows.
